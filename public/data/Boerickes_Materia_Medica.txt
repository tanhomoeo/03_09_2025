ACONITUM NAPELLUS
(Aconite)

Mind and Head
Great fear, anxiety, and worry accompany every ailment, however trivial. Delirium is characterized by unhappiness, worry, fear, raving, rarely unconsciousness. Forehead feels hot, or as if a band is around it.

Fever
High fever with a full, hard, tense pulse. Skin is dry and hot. Patient is restless and anxious. Thirst for large quantities of cold water.

Modalities
Worse, in warm room, in evening and night; from lying on affected side, from music, from tobacco-smoke, dry, cold winds. Better, in the open air.
