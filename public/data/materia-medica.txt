Example Entry:

NUX VOMICA
(Poison-nut)

Mind
Very irritable; sensitive to all impressions. Cannot bear noises, odors, light, etc. Does not want to be touched. Quarrelsome, spiteful, malicious; nervous and melancholic.

Stomach
Nausea in the morning, after eating. Sour taste, and nausea in the morning. Weight and pain in stomach; worse, eating, some time after.

Back
Backache in lumbar region. Must sit up or turn over in bed to get relief.

Sleep
Cannot sleep after 3 am; lies awake for hours; mind full of ideas; wakes up tired and weak.
