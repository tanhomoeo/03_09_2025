PULSATILLA NIGRICANS
(Wind Flower)

Mental
Weeps easily. Can hardly give her symptoms without weeping. Mild, gentle, yielding disposition. Sad, crying, despondent. Changeable mood.

Stomach
Thirstlessness with nearly all complaints. Averse to fat food, warm food, and drink. Greasy taste in the mouth. Eructations; taste of food remains a long time.

Generalities
Symptoms are ever-changing. Pains travel quickly from one part to another. Worse from heat, rich fat food, after eating, towards evening. Better in the open air, from motion, cold applications, cold food and drinks, though not thirsty.
